A New Hope

It is a period of great transition in Jack's life. Following a passion, fighting the forces of comfort and routine, he has won his first victory against the forces of Frontend Development, in a struggle that rages on.

During the battle, bugs loyal to the empire managed to enact a plan to unleash their secret weapon, the CORS error, an armored opaque response with enough power to destroy an entire project.

Pursued by the Empire’s sinister bugs, Jack races to his text editor, the millenium favicon, with plans in hand that can save his project and restore freedom to his webmaps….
